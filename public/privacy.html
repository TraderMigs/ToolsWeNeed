Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

At ToolsWeNeed.com, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you interact with our tools, including which tools you use and how often.
  • Export Data: When you export results from our tools, we record anonymous information about the export type and format.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, payment information is processed securely through our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your full payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve and optimize our tools
  • Process transactions
  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Detect and prevent fraud

Data Storage

Most of our tools operate entirely in your browser. The data you enter is processed locally and is not sent to our servers unless you explicitly choose to export or save it.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Stripe: For processing payments
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics

These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they use your information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@toolsweneed.com.
